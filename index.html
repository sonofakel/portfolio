About Me

My Background

I decided to take a course in programming because I love to be challenged on a daily basis. One of my biggest fears in life is to be bored and trapped in a job that doesn't challenge me.

I grew up in Idaho and went to the University of Idaho in Moscow. After graduating with a B.S in Marketing I found that I wanted to move to a larger city and Seattle was the place I landed.

I've bounced around Seattle doing everything from working on sailboats to hanging Chritmas light but eventually I found myself as an in-house IT guy for a small business in Ballard. This is where I started my career in Tech and I've never looked back.
